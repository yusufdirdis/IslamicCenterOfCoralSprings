ISLAMIC CENTER OF CORAL SPRINGS
مُحَمَّدٌ رَسُولُ ٱللَّٰهِ

The Islamic Center of Coral Springs (ICCS) has been serving the Muslim community in Coral Springs and surrounding areas since 2012, with a mission to provide a welcoming, spiritually nurturing, and educational environment for Muslims and non-Muslims alike. Located at 11050 Wiles Road, Suite 104, Coral Springs, FL, ICCS offers daily prayers, Friday Jumu'ah Prayer, Qur'an classes, Islamic lectures, community outreach, and interfaith dialogue. Now expanding, ICCS relies on the generosity and dedication of volunteers and donors to maintain its facilities and programs.


ICCS is more than just a mosque — it is a community for unity, spiritual growth, learning, and service. The center strives to foster an inclusive atmosphere where Muslims of diverse backgrounds feel at home, and where the broader public is invited to learn about Islam. Through educational programs, social activities, and charitable initiatives, ICCS aims to be a positive force in Broward County, promoting compassion, justice, and understanding.
